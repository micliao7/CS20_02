Our Mission

With the repair costs of glasses being so high, we wanted to create a product that would help people save money and extend the life of their glasses. Therefore, we created or specialized tape designed to put broken frames and glasses back together. Our tape is designed to be easy to use and effective at repairing glasses, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service and support.
